Good questions

Quick answers, before you ask.

What ages do you look after?
I'm comfortable with kids of all ages — from toddlers up through primary-school age. Mention your kids' ages in the booking form and I'll tailor activities to suit.
What areas do you cover?
I'm based locally and travel within a reasonable distance for bookings. Pop your address into the booking form and I'll confirm whether I can make it work.
How do I pay?
Direct deposit on the day of the booking — I'll send you the details once your booking is confirmed. Apple Pay is coming soon.
Are you certified?
Yes — I'm certified in CPR, AED and First Aid through CPD Certification Service and the Save A Life organisation. Happy to provide certificate copies on request.
How far in advance should I book?
As soon as you can! Weekend slots and party dates fill up first. That said, last-minute enquiries are always welcome — I'll let you know what's available.
Can I combine services? (e.g. face painting + party assist)
Absolutely — Party Assist already includes face painting in the hourly rate. For other combinations, just mention what you need in the booking form and I'll put together a plan.
What happens if I need to cancel?
No problem — free cancellation up to 24 hours before. After that, I appreciate as much notice as you can give so I can reorganise the day.
