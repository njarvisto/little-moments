What babysitting with me looks like

Engaged, off-screen, on-time.

Every booking starts the same way — I arrive a few minutes early, get to know your child, and have a plan for what we'll do. The goal is for your kids to be happily entertained, not parked in front of a screen.

  • I get to know your child. A quick chat at the start so I can match activities to their personality and interests.
  • Screen-free engagement. Imaginative play, creative activities, outdoor games and craft — kept off devices wherever possible.
  • Homework Club included. If your primary-age child has homework to get through, I'll sit with them and help — same hourly rate.
  • Calm with big emotions. Sibling conflicts handled patiently, behaviour corrected without damaging trust.
  • Punctual and organised. I arrive ready, with everything I need.
