Why I love this work

The little moment when they see themselves.

Face painting is special because of the moment a child looks in the mirror and sees a tiger, a butterfly, a superhero, a unicorn — and lights up. That reaction is why I do this. It's the kind of little moment parents capture in photos and kids talk about for days afterward.

I take it seriously. Designs are clean, colours are vibrant, and every child gets the time and attention to feel like the star of the moment. Quiet kids get gentle care, excited kids get matched energy. Everyone leaves the chair smiling.
