Your secret weapon for a stress-free party.

Party Assist is all about letting parents sit back and actually enjoy the day — take photos, chat with friends, engage with your child in a meaningful way — without the stress of running the games.

Book party assist → What's included
Why parents book this

Enjoy the little moments on your child's special day.

I become the kids' go-to person for the duration of the party. You point them in my direction and I'll keep them entertained, engaged, and busy with games, prizes, and activities. Up to 15 children — for the full party.

  • Free up your hands for photos. Capture the day instead of running it.
  • Actually chat with your friends. Without keeping one eye on the kids.
  • Engage meaningfully with your own child. Be present in the moments that matter.
  • One go-to person for the kids. They know who to come to with any need, all party long.
The package

What's included.

$60 / hour
For up to 15 children · Includes 3 prizes for the games
  • 3 games run by me. Pick from the list below — I bring the supplies, run the games, and keep the energy up.
  • 3 prizes included. One for the winner of each game, baked into the price.
  • Hands-on the whole party. Active for the full 2-hour party time, assisting all the children with whatever they need.
  • Set up & pack away. I arrive early to set up and stay back to pack down — you don't lift a finger.
  • Face painting bundled in at $60. Add face painting for up to 15 kids at the discounted bundle rate (saves $20 vs. the standalone $80 rate). Just tick it on the booking form.
  • Cake liaison. I work with you on cake timing — making sure games fit around the cake moment, not the other way around.
  • Cake cutting & handing out. One less thing for you to coordinate.
  • Clean up after games. I leave the space tidy.

Beyond kids' birthdays

Hosting a milestone? Bring the kids along.

Want to throw a 40th and have your friends bring their kids without anyone having to organise childcare? I'll come and entertain the kids — games, face painting, the whole thing — while you actually enjoy your milestone with your friends.

Send me the date and rough numbers and I'll put together a quote that works for the day.

Also available

Gift wrapping service — $2 per gift.

Birthday party tomorrow and the gift's still in the bag? Christmas chaos got you in a spin? Drop the presents off, I'll wrap them carefully and lovingly, and you collect them ready for the big day.

  • $2 per gift — straightforward pricing
  • 24 hours notice — drop off and collect
  • You provide the paper and gift tags
  • I provide the time, the patience, and the proper bow

Mention "gift wrapping" in the booking form with how many gifts and when you need them.
